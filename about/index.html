Hi there!!, I am Mouhamad.

I am passionate about building useful systems and software that primarily benefit underserved communities.

I am currently getting interested into storytelling and the power of language. I believe language is more than just a way to communicate. It carries history, culture, identity and knowledge. Unfortunately, in today's era, language has become a barrier for others to access knowledge, information and take advantage of what modern AI systems have to offer.

I am currently co-founding an African AI research and Technology startup where we are building speech-native models, datasets, and products that make technology accessible through natural conversation in every language and every community.
